What I Do

Sports Injury

A sports injury refers to harm or damage sustained during athletic activities or exercise. Common types include sprains, strains, fractures, dislocations, and tendon injuries. These injuries typically occur due to accidents, improper training, inadequate equipment, or insufficient warm-up.

Orthopaedic pysiotherapy

Orthopaedic physiotherapy focuses on treating musculoskeletal conditions like fractures, sprains, and joint issues. It helps restore mobility, strength, and function through exercises, manual therapy, and rehabilitation techniques.

Pain Management

Pain management involves techniques to reduce or alleviate pain, improving quality of life. It includes medications, physical therapy, nerve blocks, acupuncture, and psychological approaches like cognitive behavioral therapy.

Recover Management

Recover management focuses on strategies to support healing after injury or illness. It includes rehabilitation, rest, nutrition, physical therapy, and sometimes psychological support. The aim is to restore function, prevent complications, and promote long-term wellness.

Performance Enhancement

Performance enhancement involves improving physical, mental, or skill-based abilities to achieve optimal athletic or professional performance. Techniques include strength and conditioning, nutrition, mental training, and use of advanced technologies or equipment.

Nutrition

Nutrition is the study of how food affects the body, providing essential nutrients for energy, growth, and maintenance. A balanced diet includes carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals, supporting bodily functions and overall health.

Improving body Composition

To improve body composition, focus on strength training and cardio to build muscle and burn fat. Eat a balanced diet with sufficient protein, healthy fats, and complex carbs. Maintain a slight calorie deficit for fat loss or a surplus for muscle gain.

Correcting Fitness

Correcting fitness issues involves identifying and addressing imbalances or weaknesses in the body to improve overall function and health.

Kegal Exercise

Kegel exercises are a form of physical therapy aimed at strengthening the pelvic floor muscles, which support the bladder, uterus (in women), small intestine, and rectum.

About Me



Dr. Ajeeta Goel is a highly skilled sports physiotherapist with a passion for helping athletes and individuals recover from injuries and achieve optimal physical performance. She holds a Master’s in Sports Physiotherapy from Amity University, where she not only excelled academically but also completed intensive hands-on training with the Sports Authority of India, emerging as the top student in her class. She earned her Bachelor’s degree from GGSIPU and has accumulated valuable experience working across various hospitals and healthcare settings.

Dr. Goel’s professional journey includes esteemed associations with the Badminton Association of India and the Boxing Federation of India, where she has had the privilege of treating top-tier Indian athletes in sports such as squash, badminton, boxing, gymnastics, cycling, and tennis. Her clinical expertise spans across multiple domains, with advanced certifications in manual therapy, dry needling from South Africa, kinesiology taping from the USA, and dynamic taping from Australia.

Her practice is firmly grounded in evidence-based methodologies, and she is deeply committed to ongoing learning and professional development. Dr. Goel’s areas of interest include chronic pain management, sports rehabilitation, and recovery, ensuring that her patients not only heal but thrive in their athletic pursuits.

Beyond her professional life, Dr. Goel is a compassionate practitioner who believes in creating a comfortable, supportive environment for her patients. Her clients consistently commend her for her clear explanations, motivational approach, and personalized care. In her free time, she enjoys traveling and reading, always seeking new knowledge and experiences to enrich both her personal and professional life.

Let Dr. Ajeeta Goel be your partner in recovery, as she helps you restore strength, regain mobility, and return to peak performance.
